## About IndiaMART

IndiaMART is India's largest online B2B marketplace connecting buyers with sellers across millions of products and services.

## Key Product Areas

### Buyer Side
- **BuyLeads (BL)** — Lead generation. Buyers submit requirements, sellers receive leads.
- **Search & Discovery** — Product search, PhotoSearch/Lens (image-based), category navigation.
- **WhatsApp 9696** — AI-powered buyer chatbot. Handles queries, complaints, lead capture.

### Seller Side
- **Seller.IM** — Seller dashboard and management portal.
- **WhatsApp 8181** — Seller communication and notifications.
- **AuditMate** — Automated seller catalog auditing.
- **VANI** — Voice AI for buyer calls, capturing requirements.
- **LMS** — Lead Management System for managing BuyLeads.

### Platform
- **GLADMIN** — Internal admin tool (gladmin.intermesh.net).
- **MCAT** — Microcatalog product categorization system.
- **ISQ** — Item Searchable Quantity, seller product quality metric.

## Mailing Lists
- `marketplacelaunch@indiamart.com` — This wiki's source. Product launches, features, updates.
